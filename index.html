I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the KAIST Visual AI Group, headed by Prof. Minhyuk Sung. I earned my B.S. degree in Computer Science (Major) and Electrical Engineering (Minor) from KAIST in Aug. 2023 and my M.S. degree in Computer Science from KAIST in Feb. 2025.

My research interest encompasses various computational problems arising from the intersection of computer graphics and vision. In particular, my research area includes but is not limited to:

  • Generative Modeling for 3D Data;
  • AI for Science;
  • Multimodal 3D Data Manipulation;
  • Geometry Processing.

For details, refer to my Curriculum Vitae (PDF).
